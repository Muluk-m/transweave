**Volume orphaning risk:** Docker treats the `name:` field as the canonical volume identity. When `name:` is added to a previously unnamed volume, Docker will create NEW empty volumes on next `docker compose up`. Old implicit volumes (named something like `qlj-fe-i18n_pgdata`) become orphaned — data in them is not migrated.

**What changes:** Only the `volumes:` section at the bottom of docker-compose.yml. Service names (`postgres`, `server`, `web`) are internal service identifiers, NOT brand names — leave them unchanged.
</context>
